How to Play

Welcome!

This is a friendly betting pool for the 2026 World Cup. No real money or any forms of rewards would be given!

1. Starting Points

Every player starts with 1000 points.

Important: Your points cannot go below 0.

2. How to Place a Bet
  • Go to the Home page
  • Choose a match and enter your predicted score
  • Enter how many points you want to bet (minimum 50)
  • Click Place Bet or Update Bet
  • You can change your bet anytime before the match starts
3. How Points Are Calculated (Group Stage)
Note: This points system currently applies to Group Stage only. Knockout stage will have different (higher) multipliers.
Your Prediction Result Points Outcome
Exact Score
(e.g. predict 2-1, result is 2-1)		 Correct + Stake
(You get 2× your stake back)
Correct Winner
(e.g. predict 2-1, result is 3-1)		 Correct Winner + 0.5 × Stake
(You get 1.5× your stake back)
Wrong Prediction Incorrect - Stake
(You lose the points you bet)

Example: You bet 100 points on a Group Stage match.

  • Exact score correct → You get 200 points back (+100 net)
  • Correct winner but wrong score → You get 150 points back (+50 net)
  • Wrong → You lose 100 points

Coming Soon: Knockout Stage

4. Other Features
  • My Bets — View and delete your active bets
  • Leaderboard — See who is winning (click any name to view their bets)
  • Profile — Update your nickname, birthday, zodiac, MBTI, etc.
