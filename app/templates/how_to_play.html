How to Play

Welcome!

This is a friendly betting pool for the 2026 World Cup. No real money involved!

1. Starting Points

Every player starts with 1000 points.

Important: Your points cannot go below 0.

2. How to Place a Bet
  • Go to the Home or Knockout page
  • Choose a match and enter your predicted score
  • Enter how many points you want to bet (minimum 50)
  • Click Place Bet or Update Bet
  • You can change your bet anytime before the match starts
3. Points System - Group Stage
Your Prediction Result Points Outcome
Exact Score Correct 2× Stake
Correct Winner Correct Side 1.5× Stake
Wrong Winner Incorrect - Stake
4. Points System - Knockout Stage

Higher rewards as the tournament progresses!

Stage Exact Score Correct Winner
Round of 32 3× Stake 1.5× Stake
Round of 16 4× Stake 2× Stake
Quarterfinal 6× Stake 3× Stake
Semifinal 7× Stake 3.5× Stake
Third Place 8× Stake 4× Stake
Final 10× Stake 5× Stake

Example (Final): You bet 100 points on the Final.

  • Exact score correct → You get 1000 points back (+900 net)
  • Correct winner but wrong score → You get 500 points back (+400 net)
  • Wrong winner → You lose 100 points
5. Other Features
  • My Bets — View and delete your active bets
  • Leaderboard — See who is winning (click any name to view their profile)
  • Profile — Update your nickname, zodiac, MBTI, etc.
  • Secret Easter Eggs hidden around the site 👀
